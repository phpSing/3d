YUNPEI LI | Carry Your Life

@ppliyunpei

My inspiration comes from the artist Duane Hanson’s Sculpture "young shopper". When describing this sculpture Duane Hanson said: "I like the physical burdens this woman carries. She is weighted down by all of her shopping bags and purchases, and she has become almost a bag herself. She carries physical burdens – the burdens of life, of everyday living. But initially, it’s quite a funny sculpture". This image touched me very much. During the epidemic, I saw many people who came out of the supermarket with large bags on their shoulders, including myself. This image makes me feel very inspirational, so I really want to use the silhouette and image of carrying many bags on people’s body. The outline of the bag is used in my design. I hope to make a bag that can be easily worn. People do not have to hold and carry things bag by bag, but can wear my bag and go shopping directly.

Something can be taken home easily.
