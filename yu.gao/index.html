YU GAO | Transparent Waist Bag

@diamondkateyuu

I have always believed that fashion accessories designers can make products decorative as well as functional. The main principles of design are to better serve consumers, enhance wearers' sense of experience and comfort. Therefore, more commercial value and market will be obtained.

The name of my work is "Transparent waist bag". The outbreak of the COVID-19 makes me truly feel the power of big data. In China, positive cases are tracked in real time where they have arrived, so that the spread of the virus can be better controlled. But big data is a double-edged sword. All personal private information is stored in the data cloud, which facilitates life and makes everyone "transparent". I want to use my design to bring wearers more sense of security and experience. For most people, it is a luxury to live alone without electronic products. In my home, the "transparent bubble chair" is the most comfortable Safe Space for me. Leaning on the chair, I can read, drink tea and even meditate.

Therefore, I designed the bag at the back of the waist. The main part is composed of expansive small squares, which can be regarded as "cushion" to enhance the consumers' experience. The transparent bubble will show the colour of laser when it is affected by the environmental colour.

Therefore, the body colour is made of laser PVC and transparent PVC. The inner part of this waist bag can be seen from time to time through the splicing. This is also the so-called "In big data era, all things, there is no absolute security only relative security".
